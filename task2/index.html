About

Virat Kohli, (born November 5, 1988, Delhi, India), Indian cricketer and former captain, has played a pivotal role in shaping the nation's cricketing landscape, leading the team to numerous victories and establishing a legacy of excellence, dedication, and resilience, making him a symbol of inspiration and national pride.

“Irrespective of whether you have talent or not, one has to work hard. Just being talented doesn't mean anything; you can end up wasting it before you realize.”

--- Virat Kohli.
